For 10 waffles:

1. Put 125g warm butter in a bowl
2. Add 100g sugar
3. Add a packet of vanilla sugar
4. Mix everything
5. Add 3 eggs
6. Mix everything
7. Add 250g flour
8. Add 1TSP baking powder
9. Add a pinch of salt
10. Mix everything
11. Add 200ml milk
12. Mix everything
13. Preheat the waffle iron
14. Put dough on the waffle iron
15. Bake for 1-2 minutes
